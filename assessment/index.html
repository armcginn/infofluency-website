Why this, why now

AI is a multiplier. Point it at the right thing.

Think of AI as a multiplier. Point it at clean, connected data and it multiplies good work: faster answers, better decisions, less time lost to busywork. Point it at data that is scattered, out of date, or contradictory, and it multiplies that too, faster and more expensively than a person ever could.

So the smart first move is not buying an AI tool. It is getting a clear picture of the data you already have. That is also what your leadership needs to make good operational calls, so you solve both at once.

The good news is that most companies your size already have what they need. It is just spread across systems, spreadsheets, and a few people's heads. The AI Readiness Baseline maps that landscape, shows you where your data is solid and where it is not, and tells you honestly what has to be in place before AI can help.

AI with clean data

Faster answers, better decisions, and less time lost to busywork, multiplied.

AI with bad data

Scattered, out-of-date, contradictory data, multiplied into expensive mistakes.
