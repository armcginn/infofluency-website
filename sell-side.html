The Challenge

What buyers really evaluate

Buyers don't just evaluate how a business is performing. They evaluate how well ownership understands it.

A clean data foundation shows up in due diligence as evidence of operational maturity. It reduces the uncertainty buyers use to justify discounting valuation. It makes the EBITDA story defensible, line by line. And it cuts the surprises that drag out diligence or kill deals outright.

EBITDA discrepancies surfaced in diligence broke 21.3% of LOIs in 2025. More than double the 10.6% rate in 2023. Non-QoE diligence findings broke another 25.3%, up from 19.1%. The data issues are killing more deals, not fewer.

, Axial, 2025 Dead Deal Report

FAQs

Common questions

We're already working with a QofE firm. How does this fit?

QofE is point-in-time forensic accounting. We build the ongoing data infrastructure the QofE work depends on. The two are complementary, not duplicative. We coordinate closely with whichever firm is doing the work.

The buyer hasn't been identified yet. Is it too early?

No. Earlier is better. The work doesn't depend on a specific buyer. It depends on having clean, defensible data when any buyer arrives.

Our data is in shambles. Is six months enough?

Often, yes. The Proof Project gets the most important metrics into a working state in 30 days. The Data Partnership picks up from there. Six months is workable, twelve is better, and we've done meaningful work in less.

Can you work with our in-house finance team and accountant?

Yes. We work directly with your finance and operations people and your accountant. We handle the data and reporting build; they keep doing what they do best. No disruption to how your team already runs.

Will the buyer want to keep this infrastructure?

Frequently, yes. A working data foundation transfers cleanly to the new owner and often becomes part of the value story. Clean modern systems, defensible reporting, and a team the buyer can keep working with if they want to.

What happens if the deal doesn't close?

You keep everything. The infrastructure, the reporting, the cleaned historical data. It serves the business whether you sell next month or in three years.
