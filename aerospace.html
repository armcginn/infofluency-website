Timing

When the timing is right

There's rarely a perfect time to build. But some moments make it easier — and the payoff much bigger.

Before or just after an acquisition

Integrating a new entity is far smoother when clean data infrastructure is already in place. If it isn't, an acquisition is the ideal catalyst to build it right from the start.

When a key role is vacant or transitioning

Leadership changes create data gaps. A data foundation keeps the executive team informed on company metrics — no blind spots, no missed signals. When the right candidate is hired, everything they need is already organized and accessible.

During a software migration

If you're already moving systems, you're already disrupting the status quo. A data foundation provides continuity during the transition — ensuring data flows cleanly regardless of what's changing at the system level.

During exit preparation

Buyers want clean financials, clear operations, and evidence of a scalable infrastructure. Building your data foundation well before going to market strengthens your story, your valuation, and your confidence in the room.

If any of these moments sound familiar, a Learning Session is a good place to start.
