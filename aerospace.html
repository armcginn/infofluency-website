Client Success Story Aerospace Manufacturer

Real-Time Production Intelligence That Runs the Business

The Challenge

A precision aerospace manufacturer running production across many CNC cutting machines had no real-time visibility into their operations. Status tracking lived in spreadsheets, manually updated, always behind. Leaders were guessing. Customer service reps were estimating. And the CEO was tethered to the office just to stay informed.

What We Built

  • Real-time dashboard showing which items are complete, in progress, or on hold due to materials shortages
  • Materials inventory tracking with low-stock alerts, before shortages become disruptions
  • Available capacity visibility so the team knows exactly how many new orders they can commit to selling
  • Automated weekly reports delivered directly to major customers including Boeing
  • Auto-updated executive scorecard populated for management meetings (EOS L10s)

Management Manual report prep for every meeting Auto-updated scorecard ready for every L10
